My dearest love,

I want to tell you something I've thought about more times than I can count. I think about that walkway — the one between Queen Esther Hall and the Gazebo. On any other day, it would have just been a corridor. But it became the place where you walked into my life, even if you didn't mean to stay.

You came for a Snap. And even after we never thought we'd be more I met you outside beside the cafeteria with that black boy. You stayed as a best friend. And somewhere between all those conversations, the late nights, the laughter, and the quiet moments in between — you became the person I didn't know I was waiting for.

What I love most is that it wasn't dramatic. There was no grand moment of realisation, no fireworks (well, except maybe today 🎉). It was just you, showing up, consistently and warmly, until one day I looked up and realised you were the safest place I know.

So today, on your birthday, I want you to know this: you are deeply, genuinely loved. Not because you're perfect — but because you are so extraordinarily, beautifully you. And that version of you is my absolute favourite person in the world.

Happy birthday to my best friend, my partner, my favourite "errand" the universe ever ran. Here's to you — and to every year ahead that I get to spend knowing you. Used your favourite colour btw 🌸

With all my heart,
Your girl Mary ❤️
